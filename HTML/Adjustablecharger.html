Abstract

The adjustable charger would have three separate buttons that could change the charging speed to slow charge, regular charge, or quick charge. Batteries deteriorate over time due to overheating while charging, so slow charging could help preserve battery health by keeping the temperature down. Quick charging would allow people to temporarily increase the speed of charging but risk letting the battery get too hot.

Introduction

As mobile demands grow, a regular charger can not satisfy everyone’s desires on battery life and charging speed. To balance these two demands, we designed a charger that can charge smartphones at slow, normal, and fast speeds. Many people rely on mobile phones in their daily lives, so this charger will benefit a variety of people. If someone has limited time to charge their smartphone, they can switch the charger to quick charge so their smartphone will be charged within thirty minutes. Notice that charging in fast speed for a long time will harm the battery, users may also want to charge in slow mode during sleep time to protect their battery. Currently there are only regular chargers or quick chargers on the market. For those who want to switch between different charging speed based on situations, they have to buy two or even three chargers, which is costly and inconvenient. The adjustable charger provides different choices of charging speed, but is only the price of one charger because the inner circuit can be easily modified from a regular charger circuit.

Rationale

Long battery life and quick charging speed are two of the most favorite features for a mobile charger. However, it is hard to balance these two demands since fast chargers will raise the battery’s inner temperature thus destroying its innards. Although a slow charger can extend battery life, it requires an extended period to charge completely. To satisfy most people’s demands towards a mobile charger, we integrate these two features into a regular charger. The charging speed mostly depends on the output power from the charger. The faster the charging speed, the larger the output power is required. According to the equation P=I*V, we can alter the output power by varying voltage, current or both. We first considered changing output voltage and current instantaneously, but we could not find a practical method to increase or decrease these two parameters at the same time. Later we decided to set the output voltage to be a fixed value and only modify the output current, which can be implemented easier. We finally set the charger’s output voltage to 5V, and varies the output current from 0.5A to 2A. The output power of adjustable charger in slow mode will be 0.5A*5V=2.5W, the output of the regular charging will be 1A*5V=5W, and the output power in fast mode will be 2A*5V=10W.

Implementation

We each were assigned a topic in our area of expertise and we discussed decisions before fully implementing them. We start with a basic charger and then modify it. Rui researched how chargers were made. Mobile phones generally charge with 5V regulated DC supply, so we are going to build a 5V regulated DC supply from 220 AC. (1)The first step is to build a step down transformer to reduce such high voltage, which converts 220V AC to 10V AC. Then we need a rectifier to convert AC source to partial DC source with pulsate. Filtration is used to eliminate the pulsating output. A voltage regulator on the last part of our circuit produces regulated 5V DC. After solving the regular phone charger part, David researched how we could double the current without changing the voltage. The best way is to put two voltage regulators in parallel to double the output current in the fast charging option and put them in series to half the output current in slow charging.(2) Rebecca researched how to change between different charging mode, which can be implemented by controlling on-off switches based on the button user pressed.

Conclusion

An adjustable charger gives customers the luxury of choosing between different charging speeds. Slow charge would help prolong the battery health to keep battery life long, while quick charge would give customers convenience while they are on the go. Regular charging mixes the two by allowing smartphones to charge at a medium rate but does not increase the temperature too much. This product combines the benefits of all aspects of charging and is the next step in phone chargers.
